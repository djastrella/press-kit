DJ ASTRELLA

Groove-driven house, disco and timeless dancefloor energy

Bio

Born in Switzerland and now living between Zurich and Lisbon, DJ Astrella’s musical journey began early with classical piano and a deep fascination for harmonies. Her love for electronic music evolved through years spent dancing at clubs, afterhours and underground events, where house music became the foundation of her sound.

Known for her refined and eclectic music taste, DJ Astrella blends Soulful and Beach House with Nu‑Disco, Deep, Funky and Disco‑House, infused with 80s and 90s influences and Chillout moments. Her sets are carefully crafted journeys, moving seamlessly between styles while maintaining a warm, uplifting groove.

Whether playing at sunset on a rooftop, in an intimate bar, at a beach venue or during a corporate or private event, DJ Astrella creates an atmosphere of positive energy, connection and timeless dancefloor moments. During summer, she is most likely found sharing good vibes at beach venues or rooftops, dancing together with her crowd.
